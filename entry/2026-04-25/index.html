Chef Notes

  • ✦For extra depth, roast the tofu with a pinch of cumin or turmeric.
  • ✦If you prefer a creamier texture, add 1-2 tbsp of coconut oil to the skillet when sautéing the vegetables.

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

  2. 2

    In a bowl, toss tofu cubes with olive oil, sea salt, garlic powder, and smoked paprika. Spread in a single layer on the baking sheet.

  3. 3

    Roast tofu for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and crispy.

  4. 4

    While tofu roasts, prepare the asparagus and artichoke hearts. Trim asparagus ends and cut into 2-inch pieces. In a large skillet, heat 1 tbsp olive oil over medium heat. Add asparagus and artichoke hearts, season with sea salt, and sauté for 5-7 minutes until tender.

  5. 5

    In a small bowl, whisk together coconut milk, lemon juice, and tamari to create a creamy dressing. Set aside.

  6. 6

    Once tofu is done, transfer to a serving platter. Top with asparagus and artichoke hearts. Drizzle with the dressing and sprinkle with jalapeño for heat. Garnish with spinach leaves for color and freshness.
