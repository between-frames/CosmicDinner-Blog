Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Prepare the chicken: Pat the chicken thighs dry with paper towels. Season both sides with sea salt and set aside. In a small bowl, mix tamari, rice vinegar, and grated ginger to create a marinade. Brush the marinade evenly over the chicken thighs and let rest for 10 minutes.

  2. 2

    Steam the vegetables: In a steamer basket, add the lotus root and cucumber slices. Cover and steam for 8 minutes, until tender but still crisp. Remove from heat and set aside.

  3. 3

    Prepare the shiso infusion: In a small pot, bring 1 cup of water to a gentle simmer. Add the chopped shiso leaves and let steep for 5 minutes. Strain the infusion into a bowl, discarding the leaves. Set aside.

  4. 4

    Grill the chicken: Heat a grill pan or outdoor grill to medium-high heat. Place the chicken thighs on the grill and cook for 5 minutes per side, or until fully cooked and slightly charred. Transfer to a plate and keep warm.

  5. 5

    Combine and serve: In a serving bowl, combine the steamed lotus root and cucumber. Pour the shiso infusion over the vegetables and toss gently. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds. Serve the chicken thighs alongside the vegetable medley, and enjoy with a side of the infused shiso leaves.

  6. 6

    Chef's note: For added depth, you can briefly sear the chicken skin side down before grilling to enhance the flavor.

  7. 7

    Chef's note: If you prefer a lighter infusion, reduce the steaming time to 5 minutes for the vegetables.
