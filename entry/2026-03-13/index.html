Recipe

Fish tacos with cabbage slaw

Prep

20 min

Cook

15 min

Servings

2

Ingredients

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Prep the fish: Soak 1/2 lb white fish cubes in 1/2 cup beer for 15 mins, then drain and pat dry. (Waning Gibbous moon suggests a gentle soak for tender texture.)

  2. 2

    Make the coating: In a bowl, mix 1/4 cup flour with a pinch of salt. Dredge fish cubes in flour, shaking off excess. (Pisces’ creativity shines through in the texture contrast.)

  3. 3

    Cook the fish: Heat 1 tbsp oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add fish, cook 3-4 mins per side until golden. (Numerology 8’s balance ensures even cooking.)

  4. 4

    Prepare the slaw: In a bowl, combine 1/2 head cabbage, 1 lime’s juice, 2 tbsp mayo, and 1 minced chipotle. Toss until coated. (Zodiac Pisces’ sensitivity enhances the flavor depth.)

  5. 5

    Assemble tacos: Warm 8 corn tortillas in a skillet. Layer with fish, slaw, 1 avocado slices, and 4 radishes. Garnish with lime wedges. (Cosmic harmony achieved with each bite.)
