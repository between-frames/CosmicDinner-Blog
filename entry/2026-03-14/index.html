Ingredients

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Prep the ingredients: julienne the radish, mince the pea shoots, press and cube the tofu, and cook the pasta according to package instructions.

  2. 2

    Heat a skillet over medium-high heat, add olive oil, and sear the chicken thighs for 5-6 minutes per side until golden and cooked through, then set aside.

  3. 3

    In the same skillet, add tofu cubes and sauté for 5 minutes until golden, then stir in zested lemon and 1/4 cup lemon juice to infuse a bright aroma.

  4. 4

    Toss cooked pasta with spinach, radish, and pea shoots, then mix in cooked chicken and tofu, seasoning with sea salt and remaining lemon juice for a balanced flavor.

  5. 5

    Plate the grain bowl, drizzle with a bit of olive oil, and garnish with lemon zest for a final touch. Under the Last Quarter moon, let the Pisces energy guide your creativity—this dish is a cosmic celebration of balance and renewal.
