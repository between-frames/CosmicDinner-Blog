Chef Notes

  • ✦Swap egg for a plant-based option — the method works equally well.
  • ✦The lemon can be replaced with any bright acid: lime, yuzu, or a splash of good vinegar.

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Prep all ingredients: wash and chop asparagus and artichoke, mince morel. Have everything staged before you turn on the heat.

  2. 2

    Heat olive oil in a wide pan over medium-high heat. Season egg with salt and pepper.

  3. 3

    Toss egg and asparagus with olive oil and arrange on a baking sheet. Roast at 200°C / 400°F for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway.

  4. 4

    In the same pan, cook morel for 60 seconds until fragrant. Add asparagus and cook 4-5 minutes until just tender.

  5. 5

    Add artichoke and toss to wilt, about 2 minutes. Season with lemon juice and zest.

  6. 6

    Return egg to the pan. Toss everything together over medium heat for 1-2 minutes.

  7. 7

    Taste and adjust salt and acid. Plate with an eye to color contrast and serve immediately.

Tonight’s recipe emerged from the need to cool down after a bold Aries spice kick, using the cucumber and coconut to temper the heat. The asparagus and artichoke were fresh and peak, their seasonal timing aligned with the lunar rhythm. This dish was about balancing the fiery energy with the cooling breath of the Moon in Capricorn.
