Recipe

Charred lemon chili pasta with toasted breadcrumbs

Prep

20 min

Cook

35 min

Servings

2 servings

Ingredients

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.

  2. 2

    Char lemon halves in a skillet until deeply caramelized.

  3. 3

    Toast breadcrumbs in olive oil until golden, then season with salt.

  4. 4

    Saute garlic and chili flakes in olive oil and add pasta with reserved water.

  5. 5

    Squeeze charred lemon over pasta, toss with parmesan, and plate.

  6. 6

    Finish with toasted breadcrumbs and black pepper.
