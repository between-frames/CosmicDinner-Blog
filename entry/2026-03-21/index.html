Ingredients

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Prep: Dice chicken, julienne galangal, slice lemongrass and mushrooms, tear kaffir lime leaves, mince chilies. (Waning Gibbous energy invites precise preparation.)

  2. 2

    Heat a wok over medium-high heat, add 1 tbsp oil, then sear chicken until golden, 3-4 minutes.

  3. 3

    Add galangal and lemongrass, stir-fry 1 minute until aromatic, then pour in coconut milk and fish sauce, simmer 10 minutes.

  4. 4

    Stir in mushrooms, chilies, and lime juice; cook 5 minutes until mushrooms are tender and flavors meld.

  5. 5

    Serve hot, garnished with kaffir lime leaves and a drizzle of lime juice. (Aries' boldness pairs perfectly with this fiery yet creamy dish.)
