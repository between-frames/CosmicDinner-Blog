Chef Notes

  • ✦For a smokier flavor, use a charcoal grill.
  • ✦The jalapeño adds heat; adjust quantity based on spice preference.
  • ✦Black sesame seeds provide a nutty, cooling contrast to the lamb.
  • ✦The rice vinegar and maple syrup balance the richness of the lamb.

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Preheat grill to medium-high heat.

  2. 2

    Season lamb with sea salt and cumin.

  3. 3

    Grill lamb for 5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.

  4. 4

    While lamb grills, prepare the salad: In a bowl, combine spinach, cucumber, jalapeño, and garlic.

  5. 5

    Toss with olive oil, tamari, rice vinegar, and maple syrup.

  6. 6

    Sprinkle black sesame seeds over the salad.

  7. 7

    Serve grilled lamb with the cooling cucumber salad.

  8. 8

    If desired, drizzle with additional olive oil before serving.
