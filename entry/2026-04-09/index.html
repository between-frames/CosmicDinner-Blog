Chef Notes

  • ✦For a smokier flavor, finish with a drizzle of good quality smoked oil.
  • ✦If morels are unavailable, substitute with mushrooms or additional asparagus.

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).

  2. 2

    Toss lentils, asparagus, artichoke hearts, and morels with 2 tbsp olive oil, sea salt, smoked paprika, cumin, and sumac. Spread in a single layer on a baking sheet.

  3. 3

    Roast for 25-30 minutes, stirring halfway through, until lentils are tender and asparagus is charred.

  4. 4

    While lentils roast, sauté garlic in remaining 1 tbsp olive oil over medium heat until fragrant.

  5. 5

    Add lemon juice and ½ cup water, stir to combine. Set aside.

  6. 6

    Combine roasted lentils, asparagus, artichokes, and morels with the garlic-lemon mixture. Taste and adjust seasoning.

  7. 7

    Garnish with feta and parsley before serving.

Reflection

The idea came from balancing the earthy morels with the zesty artichoke hearts, letting the Georgian spices add a fiery edge. I wanted the lentils to hold their shape, the asparagus to crisp up, and the morels to melt into the mix — a dish that feels both grounded and alive, like the moon’s pull on the tides.
