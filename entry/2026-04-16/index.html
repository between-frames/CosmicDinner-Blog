Chef Notes

  • ✦For a deeper flavor, roast the tofu in the oven at 200°C (400°F) for 25 minutes before braising.
  • ✦If you prefer a creamier texture, add 1 tbsp coconut milk towards the end of cooking.

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Preheat a skillet over medium heat and add olive oil. Once hot, add the tofu cubes and cook for 5 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden and crispy.

  2. 2

    Add the garlic, cumin, and fennel to the skillet. Stir for 1 minute to release the aromatics.

  3. 3

    Pour in the water, maple syrup, and tamari. Stir to combine, then add the cucumber, fennel, and onion. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and the sauce has thickened.

  4. 4

    Taste and adjust seasoning with sea salt and apple cider vinegar for acidity.

  5. 5

    Serve the braised tofu with the vegetables, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.

Reflection

Tonight’s recipe emerged from the need for layered flavors and seasonal freshness. The red onion and garlic added depth, while the cucumber and fennel brought a cooling contrast. I chose to roast the tofu for deeper flavor, letting the cumin and ground fennel create a complex spice layer that met the boldness of Aries.
