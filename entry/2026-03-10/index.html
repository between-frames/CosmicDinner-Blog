Recipe

Teff injera bowl with lentils

Prep

10 min

Cook

40 min

Servings

2

Ingredients

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Prep the teff batter: In a bowl, mix 1/2 cup teff flour with 1 cup warm water, stirring until smooth. Let rest 10 minutes.

  2. 2

    Sauté base: Heat 2 tbsp niter kibbeh in a skillet over medium heat. Add 1 small onion, sauté 5 minutes until softened.

  3. 3

    Build flavor: Stir in 2 cloves garlic and 1/2 inch ginger, cooking 1 minute. Add 1 tbsp berbere, toasting 30 seconds.

  4. 4

    Cook lentils: In a pot, combine 1/2 cup red lentils with 4 cups water. Bring to a boil, then simmer 20 minutes until tender.

  5. 5

    Assemble bowl: In a bowl, mix cooked lentils with 2 medium diced tomatoes and 3 cups chopped spinach. Season with salt.

  6. 6

    Cook injera: Pour teff batter into a non-stick pan, spread evenly. Cook 3-4 minutes per side until puffed and golden.

  7. 7

    Plate with cosmic flair: Serve injera as a base, topped with lentil mixture. Add a sprinkle of niter kibbeh for a touch of Pisces' intuitive depth.
