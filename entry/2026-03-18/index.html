Recipe

Avocado toast with poached egg

Prep

10 min

Cook

5 min

Servings

2

Ingredients

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Toast sourdough slices until golden, 5-7 minutes.

  2. 2

    In a small bowl, mash avocado with a fork until creamy, adding 1 tbsp lemon juice for brightness.

  3. 3

    In a saucepan, poach eggs: bring water to a simmer, gently lower eggs, cook 6 minutes for runny yolks.

  4. 4

    Season poached eggs with chili flakes, a pinch of salt, and a dash of black pepper.

  5. 5

    Arrange toast on plate, top with avocado spread, place poached egg on avocado, garnish with microgreens and remaining lemon zest.

  6. 6

    Serve immediately, savoring the harmony of earthy sourdough and creamy avocado under the First Quarter moon’s gentle pull.
