Ingredients

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Soak sweet potato noodles in warm water for 10 minutes until pliable, then drain and set aside.

  2. 2

    Heat 1 tsp sesame oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat; add beef slices and sear for 3-4 minutes until browned, then set aside.

  3. 3

    In the same wok, add 1/2 tsp sesame oil and sauté garlic for 30 seconds until fragrant, then add julienned carrots and mushrooms; cook for 2-3 minutes until tender.

  4. 4

    Add onion and spinach to the wok; stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until spinach wilts and onion softens.

  5. 5

    Stir in soaked noodles, soy sauce, sugar, and cooked beef; mix well to coat, cooking for 2-3 minutes until heated through and flavors meld.

  6. 6

    Taste and adjust seasoning if needed; serve immediately, garnished with a drizzle of sesame oil for a touch of cosmic balance.

  7. 7

    Under the full moon’s glow, let the dish’s sweetness mirror the Pisces’ dreamy essence, perfect for a numerologically harmonious 6.
