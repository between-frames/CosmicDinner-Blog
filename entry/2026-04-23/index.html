Lunar Kaiseki Baked Chicken Thighs with Cucumber & Lotus Root

Chef Notes

  • ✦For a deeper flavor, you can baste the chicken with the glaze during the last 10 minutes of roasting.
  • ✦If you prefer a more intense ginger flavor, increase the amount of minced ginger to 1.5 tsp.
  • ✦The lotus root adds a subtle bitterness that complements the sweetness of the cucumber.
  • ✦To enhance the cooling effect, you can serve with a side of chilled tofu or a light miso soup.

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).

  2. 2

    Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towels and season with sea salt and tamari.

  3. 3

    Place in a baking dish and drizzle with olive oil.

  4. 4

    Roast for 25 minutes, then flip and roast another 10 minutes until golden and cooked through.

  5. 5

    While chicken roasts, prepare the cucumber and lotus root by slicing and julienning.

  6. 6

    In a small bowl, mix rice vinegar, mirin, minced ginger, and minced garlic to make a glaze.

  7. 7

    Once chicken is done, arrange cucumber and lotus root on top.

  8. 8

    Drizzle the glaze over the vegetables and serve immediately.

  9. 9

    Optional: Garnish with additional ginger or scallions for extra flavor.
