Chef Notes

  • ✦For a more intense flavor, roast the chickpeas in the oven at 200°C (400°F) for 15 minutes before steaming.
  • ✦If you prefer a creamier texture, add a splash of coconut milk to the steamed vegetables before serving.

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Steam the bitter chickpeas, cucumber, asparagus, artichoke hearts, and spinach in a steamer basket over boiling water for 15-20 minutes, until tender but still firm.

  2. 2

    In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, sea salt, cumin, lemon juice, and smoked paprika to make the dressing.

  3. 3

    Once the vegetables are steamed, transfer them to a serving bowl.

  4. 4

    Pour the dressing over the vegetables and toss to coat.

  5. 5

    Garnish with the grated ginger and minced garlic just before serving.

Reflection

The recipe came together from the need for cooling hydration and bold spice—cucumber and coconut offered relief, while roasted chickpeas brought a smoky heat that cut through the greens. Iftar traditions inspired the warming heart of the meal, but the Ayurvedic balance of sweet and bitter made it feel like a full, shared plate.
