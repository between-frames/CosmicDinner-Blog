Servings

Ingredients

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Chop spinach and drain excess liquid; cube paneer for even cooking.

  2. 2

    In a pan, heat 1 tbsp ghee, add diced onion and sauté until golden (5 mins), stirring to release sweetness.

  3. 3

    Add minced garlic and grated ginger, stir for 1 min until aromatic.

  4. 4

    Stir in diced tomato, cook until softened (5 mins), then add cumin seeds and garam masala.

  5. 5

    Add chopped spinach, cook 2 mins until wilted; stir in cream, simmer 3 mins until thickened.

  6. 6

    Garnish with a pinch of garam masala, serve with rice. (Pisces’ intuitive touch enhances the dish’s soulful balance.)
