Recipe

Grilled halloumi and tabbouleh

Prep

Cook

10 min

Servings

2

Ingredients

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Prep the tabbouleh by cooking bulgur according to package instructions, then draining and letting cool (20 mins).

  2. 2

    In a bowl, combine bulgur, parsley, mint, tomato, cucumber, scallion, and lemon juice; mix gently to coat (5 mins).

  3. 3

    Heat a grill pan over medium-high heat, then lightly oil the surface (2 mins).

  4. 4

    Place halloumi cubes on the grill, pressing gently to seal in juices; cook 3-4 mins per side until golden and slightly charred (6 mins).

  5. 5

    Assemble by placing tabbouleh in bowls, topping with grilled halloumi, and drizzling with remaining olive oil. Serve while warm, under the waxing gibbous moon, which symbolizes growth and creativity—perfect for nurturing your Pisces intuition with numerology 6’s harmonious energy.
