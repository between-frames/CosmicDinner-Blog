Chef Notes

  • ✦For a vegan option, substitute honey with maple syrup.
  • ✦If strawberries are not in season, use frozen strawberries thawed and drained.
  • ✦Adjust the amount of jalapeño based on desired heat level.

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Preheat a steamer or bring a pot of water to a boil for blanching.

  2. 2

    Steam the white beans for 10 minutes or blanch in boiling water for 2 minutes, then drain and set aside.

  3. 3

    In a large bowl, combine the cooked white beans, cucumber, snap peas, and spinach.

  4. 4

    Toss with coconut oil, sea salt, ginger, garlic powder, and tamari.

  5. 5

    In a separate bowl, whisk together coconut milk, lime juice, and honey until smooth.

  6. 6

    Pour the dressing over the salad and mix well.

  7. 7

    Divide the salad between two plates.

  8. 8

    Top with strawberries, chopped mint, and sliced almonds.

  9. 9

    Serve immediately for optimal freshness.

Reflection

Tonight’s dish emerged from a balance of bold simplicity and playful duality, shaped by the Gemini’s need for contrast and the waxing moon’s cooling energy. The white beans provided a grounding base, while the strawberries and cucumber added a bright, sweet counterpoint. It was a decision rooted in seasonal peak produce and the need for layered complexity without overwhelming the senses.
