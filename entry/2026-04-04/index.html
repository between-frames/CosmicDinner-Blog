Scorpio Moon Kaiseki: Pan-Seared Salmon with Asparagus & Morels

Prep

15 min

Cook

Servings

2

Ingredients

Protein

Produce

Aromatics

Pantry

Garnish

Chef Notes

  • ✦For a more intense flavor, roast the asparagus and morels together for 15 minutes, then finish with a splash of mirin for a subtle sweetness.
  • ✦If the salmon is too dry, add a small amount of mirin or a splash of water to the pan during roasting.

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. 2

    Prepare the salmon: Pat the salmon fillets dry with paper towels. Season both sides with sea salt and a pinch of black pepper. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the salmon and sear for 3-4 minutes per side, or until golden brown and just cooked through. Transfer to a baking sheet and roast for 8-10 minutes, or until fully cooked. Set aside.

  3. 3

    Prepare the asparagus and morels: Toss the asparagus with a drizzle of coconut oil and a pinch of sea salt. Roast in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until tender and slightly charred. In a separate pan, sauté the morels with a bit of butter (or coconut oil) over medium heat for 2-3 minutes, until softened and fragrant.

  4. 4

    Make the artichoke glaze: In a small bowl, whisk together tamari, mirin, and a splash of water. Pour the glaze over the artichoke hearts and let sit for 5 minutes to absorb the flavors.

  5. 5

    Assemble the dish: Place the roasted asparagus on a serving plate. Top with the sautéed morels and artichoke hearts. Arrange the pan-seared salmon on top. Garnish with shredded cucumber and toasted sesame seeds. Serve immediately.

  6. 6

    Chef's note: For an extra burst of flavor, add a drizzle of soy sauce or a squeeze of lime just before serving.
