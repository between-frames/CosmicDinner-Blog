Chef Notes

  • ✦For a smokier flavor, finish with a splash of smoked paprika or a few drops of liquid smoke.
  • ✦If using a regular oven, ensure the skillet is oven-safe for even cooking.

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. 2

    In a large oven-safe skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Add lamb shoulder and sear for 3-4 minutes per side until browned. Remove and set aside.

  3. 3

    Add ginger and garlic to the skillet, sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.

  4. 4

    Stir in lentils, spinach, jalapeño, and watercress. Cook for 2-3 minutes until wilted.

  5. 5

    Pour in coconut milk, tamari, maple syrup, apple cider vinegar, and water. Stir to combine.

  6. 6

    Return lamb to the skillet and transfer to oven. Roast for 20-25 minutes until lamb is tender and lentils are softened.

  7. 7

    Remove from oven and let rest for 5 minutes.

  8. 8

    Serve with a sprinkle of cilantro and green onions.
