Chef Notes

  • ✦For a deeper umami flavor, you can roast the asparagus in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for 10 minutes before adding it to the pan.
  • ✦If you prefer a lighter glaze, reduce the amount of miso paste by half and add a bit more mirin for sweetness.

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Preheat a grill to medium-high heat.

  2. 2

    Season the salmon with sea salt and olive oil, then place on the grill. Grill for 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

  3. 3

    In a separate pan, heat coconut oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, sauté for 1 minute.

  4. 4

    Add the asparagus and cook for 3-4 minutes, until tender. Add a splash of dashi broth and cook for another 2 minutes.

  5. 5

    In a small bowl, mix the miso paste, tamari, mirin, and a splash of dashi broth to create a glaze.

  6. 6

    Once the salmon is cooked, brush the glaze over the top.

  7. 7

    Meanwhile, cook the quinoa according to package instructions, adding the sea salt during cooking.

  8. 8

    Once the quinoa is cooked, fluff it with a fork and transfer to a serving bowl.

  9. 9

    Assemble the dish by placing the quinoa as the base, then the asparagus, and finally the grilled salmon on top.

  10. 10

    Garnish with toasted sesame seeds and green onions before serving.
