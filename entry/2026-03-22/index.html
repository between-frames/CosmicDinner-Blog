Recipe

Cosmic comfort noodle soup

Prep

Cook

22 min

Servings

2

Ingredients

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Soak rice noodles in warm water for 10 minutes until pliable, drain and set aside.

  2. 2

    In a large pot, sauté diced onion in 1 tbsp oil over medium heat until translucent, 5 minutes.

  3. 3

    Add sliced mushrooms and grated ginger, cook until mushrooms release moisture and soften, 8 minutes.

  4. 4

    Pour in 4 cups chicken broth, bring to a gentle simmer, then stir in 1/4 cup uncooked rice and 1/2 cup cooked beans.

  5. 5

    Cook rice in broth for 15 minutes, or until rice is tender and broth is absorbed, stirring occasionally.

  6. 6

    Stir in fresh spinach until wilted, about 2 minutes, then add julienned radish for a crisp contrast.

  7. 7

    Taste and adjust seasoning, serve bowls hot with a cosmic note: 'Last Quarter Moon’s energy invites you to savor nourishment and embrace your Aries courage.']}
