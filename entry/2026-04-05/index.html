Chef Notes

  • ✦Swap chicken thighs for a plant-based option — the method works equally well.
  • ✦The lemon can be replaced with any bright acid: lime, yuzu, or a splash of good vinegar.

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Prep all ingredients: wash and chop asparagus and artichokes, mince black sesame. Have everything staged before you turn on the heat.

  2. 2

    Heat olive oil in a wide pan over medium-high heat. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper.

  3. 3

    Cook chicken thighs until done through (internal temp 74°C / 165°F for poultry, golden for plant protein). Remove and set aside.

  4. 4

    In the same pan, cook black sesame for 60 seconds until fragrant. Add asparagus and cook 4-5 minutes until just tender.

  5. 5

    Add artichokes and toss to wilt, about 2 minutes. Season with lemon juice and zest.

  6. 6

    Return chicken thighs to the pan. Toss everything together over medium heat for 1-2 minutes.

  7. 7

    Taste and adjust salt and acid. Plate with an eye to color contrast and serve immediately.
