Recipe

Avgolemono

Prep

10 min

Cook

25 min

Servings

2

Ingredients

Cooking Steps

  1. 1

    Prep: In a small bowl, whisk together 2 large eggs, 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and 1/8 teaspoon white pepper until frothy. (New Moon energy enhances clarity in cooking.)

  2. 2

    Heat: In a medium saucepan, bring 1/2 cup chicken broth to a gentle simmer over medium heat.

  3. 3

    Build flavor: Add 1/4 cup uncooked rice to the simmering broth, stirring to combine. Let cook for 5 minutes, then stir in 1/2 cup shredded cooked chicken.

  4. 4

    Cook: Slowly pour the egg-lemon mixture into the rice-broth-chicken mixture while stirring constantly to avoid curdling. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the mixture thickens and becomes creamy.

  5. 5

    Plate: Garnish with 1 teaspoon fresh dill, minced. Serve immediately while warm, savoring the Pisces' intuitive connection to flavor.
